'-' = floor
'#' = Wall
'*' = Spikes
'~' = Stairs to next floor
'=' = Spawn point for floor: draws as wall in game
'$' = Game end point
'@' = Coin Spawn
'"' = Health Spawn
'+' = Armor Spawn
'!' = Plasmoid Spawn
'?' = Construct Spawn
'&' = Goblin Spawn
Anything else gets drawn as is, in the colors of the walls.
